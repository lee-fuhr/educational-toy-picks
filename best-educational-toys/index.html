1. LEGO Technic Simple Machines Set 44014: the do-it-all mechanical engineering foundation

Pros

  • Teaches gears, levers, and mechanical advantage without screens

Best for specific needs

Best for hands-on mechanical learning

If your goal is to help a child understand how gears, levers, and pulleys actually work in the real world, the LEGO Technic Simple Machines Set is the undisputed champion. It’s the kind of toy that rewards patient tinkering and sparks questions like “Why does this gear make it slower?” It pairs perfectly with a curious mind that wants to build trucks, cranes, and catapults from first principles. Our pick: LEGO Technic Simple Machines Set 44014.

Best for screen-free coding for young kids

For the 4-to-6 crowd, the Learning Resources Coding Critters (Ranger) is a standout. It uses a storybook adventure to teach sequencing without a screen, and the physical coding cards make the concept concrete. Kids don’t realize they’re learning logic, they just see a little dog responding to their commands. Our pick: Learning Resources Coding Critters Ranger.

Best for interactive learning with a tablet

When you’re ready to blend digital and physical play, the Osmo Genius Starter Kit delivers an experience that feels modern and genuinely adaptive. It’s especially strong for kids who need a bit of variety across subjects, math, spelling, and visual problem-solving all in one ecosystem. Just be sure you have the iPad to run it. Our pick: Osmo Genius Starter Kit.

Our verdict

No single toy covers every aspect of STEM, but the LEGO Technic Simple Machines Set comes closest for all-around mechanical understanding and open-ended fun. If robotics is the goal, grab the Thames & Kosmos Robot Factory; for the youngest coders, the Learning Resources Coding Critters is a gem. Budget shoppers will love Snap Circuits Jr., and tablet-based families should spring for the Osmo Genius Starter Kit. Match the pick to your child’s age and curiosity, and you’ll have a gift that keeps teaching long after the initial excitement fades.
